Bio

MultiStyle Violist, Violinist, and Singer. She graduated in musical performance at Madrid Royal Conservatoire of Music. She started her musical studies under the tutelage of Remei Silvestre Vinaixa, and has received lessons from exceptional mentors such as Alan Kovacs, Thuan Do Minh, Jaime Huertas, Laure Gaudron, Abel and Arnau Tomàs, Helena Poggio, and Mikhail Zemtsov throughout her career.

After completing her M.A. in Spanish Music Performance in July 2021, she received a Presidential Scholarship to pursue another Master's degree at New Jersey City University. At NJCU, she furthered her studies in music production, jazz, and modern music under the guidance of Martha Mooke, Joe Deninzon, and Regina Carter.

She is currently an adjunct professor at New Jersey City University and works with various musical groups including New York Arabic Orchestra, Swing Machine Orchestra, Spanish Philharmonic Orchestra, Multicultural Music Orchestra, and Madrid Philharmonic Orchestra.

She has performed for renowned companies including Arizona, Netflix, Nike, Tiffany & Co, Ubisoft, the U.S. Open Golf Championship, and VOGUE in over 19 different countries. She has performed in some of the world's most prestigious venues, including the United Nations Headquarters in New York, the Spanish and Mexican National Auditoriums, New Jersey’s Prudential Center, Madrid’s Wizink Center and Royal Theatre, Barcelona's L'Auditori, Bogota's Royal Center, and Chile's Teatro Caupolican, among others.

As a professional studio musician, she has recorded film and video game soundtracks all over the globe, including titles such as Assassin's Creed Mirage, G.E.O, Miocardio, and Sentimos las Molestias.

In 2020, she co-founded SciFi Ensemble with violinist Naxo Lara, an acoustic-electric string quintet with drums specializing in Video Game, Film, and Anime music. In the world of modern music, she has performed and recorded with artists and projects like Alexia Bomtempo, Blas Cantó, El Dúo Dinámico, Destripando la Historia, ELE, Film Symphony Orchestra, IL Divo, Isabel Pantoja, Joan Manuel Serrat, Laura Marie, Paloma San Basilio, Pitingo, Rahzel, Swing Machine Orchestra, The American ELO, and Yo Soy Raton.
